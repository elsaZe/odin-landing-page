Some random bee facts.
Bees are pollinators. This means they collect pollen from plants to make honey.
Honeybees live in colonies in structures called hives.
Bumblebees do not live in hives. They are solitary bees.
When a hive gets too crowded some bees move out. This is called a swarm.
